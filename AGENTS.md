# Game HUD Terms

Use these terms consistently in this workspace:

- `FIELD`: the two 5-card spaces in the middle of the game HUD. This is where active or played cards are placed.

- `HAND`: the player's own cards shown at the bottom part of the screen. These are cards drawn from the player's deck and available to play when eligible.

- `OPPONENT HAND`: the opponent's cards shown at the top part of the screen.

When discussing layout or gameplay, do not mix up `FIELD`, `HAND`, and `OPPONENT HAND`.
